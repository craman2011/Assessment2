What is Cyberbullying?

Signs that someone may be being cyberbullyied:

  • Avoiding the computer, Mobile phone or other devices
  • Looking worried when receiving e-mails or text
  • Withdrawing from family and friends
  • Not wanting to go to school
  • Avoiding talking about computer use
  • signs of low self-esteem including depression or fear
  • Bad grades
  • Bad eating or sleeping habits


Cyber Ethics

Before you post, think!

  • T – Is it True?
  • H – Is it Helpful?
  • I – Is it inspiring?
  • N – Is it Necessary?
  • K – Is it Kind?
